Plastic Waste Reduction

Exploring student-friendly ways to reduce and repurpose plastic on campus and in our community.

What We Have Done

Audit & Analysis

Conducted comprehensive audits to quantify plastic flux and identify consumption hotspots within campus operations. Utilised data-driven insights to design targeted intervention strategies that minimised single-use plastic at the source.

Reuse Streams

Established specialised collection points to segregate waste into high-quality feedstock for local manufacturing. Transformed discarded plastics into functional campus tools, effectively closing the resource loop and extending material lifecycles.

Collaborative Prototyping

Partnered with local makers to co-design and prototype innovative products based on circular economy principles. Validated the feasibility of small-scale, decentralised recycling solutions through real-world pilot testing.
