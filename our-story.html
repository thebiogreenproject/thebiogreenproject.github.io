Our Story

How it began, what we learned, and where we’re headed.

How it started

From a small student project to reuse brewery spent grain as fertiliser, we started testing with broad beans on local allotments, learning from growers and brewers along the way.

What we learned

We found promise in using spent grain to support soil structure and plant growth. The bigger lesson? Community beats lab walls: students, brewers, and gardeners together create practical sustainability.

Where we’re headed

We’re building a collaborative network across universities and communities, sharing methods, seeds, and stories. If you’d like to join or replicate this project, reach out — we’re happy to help you get started.
