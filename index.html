Welcome

We’re turning brewery waste into greener growth.

A student‑led sustainability movement at the University of Nottingham. Join us to build a community of brewers, growers, and students experimenting with spent grain for better soils and stronger crops.

Our Story in a Nutshell

Biogreen started as a small student experiment to reuse brewery spent grain as fertiliser — now it’s a growing network of students, brewers, and gardeners building sustainable futures together.
